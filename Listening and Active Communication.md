# Listening and Active Communication

#### Question 1
What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening? (Minimum 6 points)
- Be focused on the speaker, Avoid distractions
- Let the speaker finish his/her speech before asking questions
- Be open-minded
- Show any signs of interest in the talk (Non-verbal cues, such as nodding, maintaining eye contact, and leaning slightly forward)
- Summarize the talk for a better understanding
- Take notes, which can be used as reference while asking questions afterwards

#### Question 2
According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening? (Write in your own words, use simple English)
- Taking notes of key details
- Paraphrasing and validating with the speaker
- Share the feelings of the speaker
- Show that you are interested in the talk
- Be interactive, Do not make it a one-way communication

#### Question 3
What are the obstacles in your listening process?
- Fewer interactions with the speaker
- Jump to conclusions without listening fully
- Get distracted in the middle

#### Question 4
What can you do to improve your listening?
- Practice active listening
- Be Interactive
